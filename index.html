Instructions

Click ships on the left to rotate them between horizontal and vertical. Drag them onto the board to place them.

You have your first 3 turns to place your ships as the AI does the same. After that, you can shoot at a square once per turn to try and hit the AI ships, while it tries to hit yours. The current turn state is indicated at the top of the board.

The first one to hit all of the enemy ships is the winner.
